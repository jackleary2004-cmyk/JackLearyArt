GRANITE CHIEF
An exploratory booklet tracing the story of Granite Chief Peak — a place defined by altitude, history, and the kind of silence you only find above the treeline. Through photography, typography, and layout, the project translates landscape into language, turning a single peak in Olympic Valley, California into a narrative worth sitting with.
