* NOT A SURF WAX BRAND
PROJECT BRIEF

Overview
Puck + Chuck is a surf-culture brand built around a magnetic key stash disguised as a surf wax puck. It blends into a beach bag or surf kit so it doesn't draw attention like a traditional lockbox, with the option to magnetically attach to the underside of a car.

The Problem
Vehicle theft targeting surfers is a documented, ongoing pattern — most surfers are currently betting on luck. There is no existing product that is both discreet and purpose-built for key security in a surf context.

The Solution
A two-piece plastic container molded to match the shape, size, and feel of a standard surf wax puck, indistinguishable from the real thing. Its magnetic top allows it to attach hidden under a vehicle as a secondary option.

Design Approach
The project followed a human-centered design process, with the designer drawing on personal experience as a surfer to identify the gap and prototype around the concept of security through disguise. User feedback was collected and was strongly positive.

Brand Identity
The brand draws on the visual language of vintage surf culture — bold chunky lettering, high-contrast color palettes across multiple colorways, and kraft box packaging that reads as authentic surf product. The goal is for Puck + Chuck to look like something you'd find in a board shop, not a security aisle.
